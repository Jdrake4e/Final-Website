About Me:

I am a 22-year-old University of Pittsburgh student. A diversity of subjects ranging from politics, bushcraft, agriculture, engineering, mathematics, and many more hold my attention. I am ever curious about the world due to the fascinating interconnectivity of subjects. I consistently aim to learn and explore the world through travel and study.

Hobbies:

I have several Hobbies but the three that capture my time, video gaming, hiking, and cooking. I am provided great insight through the manner I interact with each hobby.

Video Games:

When I play video games I spend more time theory-crafting, spreadsheeting, and troubleshooting my modifications than playing the game. Furthermore, I study in-game economies. Additionally, I find the process of troubleshooting incredibly satisfying, signified by my ten-plus-year history of modding Minecraft.
